How We Engage

Two Ways to Work With Us

Contingency Search
Direct Placement

You pay only when we successfully place a candidate. Ideal for defined roles where you want to evaluate top talent before committing.

  • No upfront fee — results-only billing
  • Active candidate sourcing begins immediately
  • Typically 3–5 vetted candidates delivered
  • Full replacement guarantee included
  • Best for: individual contributor to manager-level roles
Retained Search
Executive Retained

A dedicated, exclusive partnership for senior and hard-to-fill roles. We commit full resources to your search with structured milestones.

  • Exclusive engagement — full team dedicated to you
  • Comprehensive market mapping included
  • Regular progress reporting and insights
  • Best for: Director, VP, and C-suite searches
  • Also available as container (partial retainer)
